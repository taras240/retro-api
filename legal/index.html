Last updated: June 7, 2025

This policy explains what personal data Retrocheevos collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

When you sign in with Google, we receive the following data from your Google account:

  • Display name — used to personalize your in-app experience
  • Email address — used to uniquely identify your account
  • Profile picture — displayed in the app interface

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or any data beyond what is listed above.

Your data is used exclusively to operate the RetroСheevos application — to authenticate your account, save your achievement tracking progress, and display your profile. We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, a cloud platform operated by Google LLC. Firebase stores account information and achievement data on Google's servers in accordance with Google's security and privacy standards.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for any commercial purpose.

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and data storage (privacy policy)
  • Google OAuth 2.0 — sign-in authentication (privacy policy)

Data retention & deletion

Your data is retained for as long as you maintain an active account. You may request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Children's privacy

RetroСheevos is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete the data promptly.
